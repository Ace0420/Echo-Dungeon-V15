## üéØ Essential Commands

### Character Creation
- **Male / Female / Non-binary** - Choose gender
- **Human / Elf / Dwarf / Orc** - Choose race  
- **Warrior / Mage / Rogue** - Choose class
- **[Your Name]** - Name your hero

### Movement
- **North** / **Forward** - Move north
- **South** / **Back** - Move south
- **East** / **Right** - Move east
- **West** / **Left** - Move west

### Exploration
- **Look around** - Describe current room
- **Search room** - Find hidden items
- **Open chest** - Loot treasure
- **Drink fountain** - Full heal + mana restore (once per floor)
- **Use shrine** - Random permanent blessing
- **Use stairs** - Descend to next floor
- **Merchant** - Open shop

### Combat
- **Attack** - Basic attack
- **Defend** - 50% damage reduction this turn
- **Special** - Use class ability
- **Cast [spell name]** - Cast learned spell
- **Use health potion** / **Use mana potion** - Drink potions
- **Flee** - Attempt escape (can't flee from bosses!)

### Equipment & Progression
- **Equip [item name]** - Equip gear from inventory
- **Equip ring [ring name]** - Equip ring (max 10 total)
- **Remove ring [ring name]** - Unequip ring
- **Read book [spell name]** - Learn spell from book
- **Meditate** - Restore 10 mana √ó your level

### Information
- **Status** - Current stats
- **Inventory** - List items
- **Help** - Command list
- **Hint** - Random game tip

### Save System
- **Save** - Save game (saves as your character name)
- **Load [name]** - Load saved character
- **List saves** - See all saved characters
- **Delete save [name]** - Remove saved character

## üí∞ Merchant Strategy

### Essential Early Purchases
1. **Ring of Regeneration** (400g) - Buy as many as possible! Each heals 50/turn
2. **Elixir of Immortality** (400g) - Auto-revive once at 50% health
3. **Potion of Giant Strength** (300g) - Double damage for 3 battles
4. **Health/Mana Potions** - Stock up for tough fights

### Mid-Game Power Spikes
1. **Merchant's Lucky Coin** (500g) - +50% gold from all sources
2. **Crystal of Experience** (600g) - +100% XP gain
3. **Better Equipment** - Class-specific weapons and armor

### Late-Game Must-Haves
1. **Charm of Fortune** (1500g) - 2x gold AND 2x XP!
2. **Ring of Spell Storing** (2000g) - 25% spell cost reduction
3. **Legendary Equipment** - Endgame weapons and armor
4. **Supreme/Ultimate Potions** - Major healing

### Pro Tips
- By level 10, have at least 5 Rings of Regeneration (250 healing/turn!)
- Drink Elixir of Immortality before boss fights
- Stack Lucky Coin + Crystal + Charm for maximum gains
- Save gold for equipment upgrades on deeper floors

## üó∫Ô∏è Navigation Tips

- **Starting Position**: Always 6,6 (center)
- **Boss Location**: Always 11,11 (far southeast corner)

## üé≤ Progression Guide

### Levels 1-5: Early Game
- Focus on survival
- Buy health/mana potions
- Get 2-3 Rings of Regeneration
- Learn your class abilities
- Explore carefully

### Levels 6-10: Mid Game  
- Start stacking Rings of Regeneration (aim for 5+)
- Buy Lucky Coin and Crystal of Experience
- Upgrade to Greater potions
- Learn spell books (mages)
- Challenge the boss!

### Levels 11-20: Late Game
- Stack 8-10 Rings of Regeneration
- Get Charm of Fortune for max efficiency
- Supreme/Ultimate potions only
- Legendary equipment
- Deep floor diving

### Levels 21+: Endgame
- Infinite scaling begins
- Every 10 floors: damage multiplier increases
- Perfect your build
- How deep can YOU go?
